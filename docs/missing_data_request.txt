DATA REQUEST FOR ANALYTICS BASELINE - FETTLE CONNECT

To provide accurate ROI and Financial Intelligence reports, we require the following baseline data from the hospital:

1. NO-SHOW BASELINE
   - Current percentage of patients who book an appointment but do not attend (Pre-Fettle implementation).
   - This is used to calculate "No-Show Reduction Revenue Recovered".

2. HR & OPERATIONAL COSTS
   - Average monthly salary/payroll cost for a front-desk/calling staff member.
   - Average hours spent per day by staff on manual follow-up calls.
   - This is used to calculate "Cost Efficiency Value" and "Equivalent FTE Freed".

3. REVENUE ASSUMPTIONS
   - Average revenue per Outpatient (OPD) appointment (if different from the ₹650 average calculated from the doctor list).
   - Average conversion rate from a missed call to a booked appointment.

4. HISTORICAL INTERACTION DATA
   - Total number of inbound calls received monthly before Fettle.
   - Current "Call Answer Rate" by human staff.

Please provide this data in an Excel or text format so we can calibrate the analytics engine.
